# 🇮🇳 Indian Culture & Festival App

A beautifully designed mobile application that showcases the rich heritage, traditions, and festivals of India.  
This project is created in **Figma** as a UI/UX case study.

## 📱 Project Overview

The Indian Culture & Festival App is designed to:

- Showcase major Indian festivals
- Provide cultural information
- Display traditional attire & food
- Include state-wise cultural highlights
- Offer a modern and clean user experience

---

## 🎯 Target Users

- Students
- Tourists
- Culture Enthusiasts
- Event Organizers
- Schools & Colleges
